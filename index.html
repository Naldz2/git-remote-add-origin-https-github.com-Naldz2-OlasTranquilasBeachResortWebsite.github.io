Olas Tranquilas Introduction

Welcome to Olas Tranquilas Beach Resort, where the soothing rhythm of the waves meets the epitome of coastal luxury. Nestled along the pristine shores of Roxas Palawan, our exclusive resort is a haven of relaxation and rejuvenation, inviting you to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and embrace the tranquility of a beachfront paradise.

Olas Tranquilas Mission

Our mission at Olas Tranquilas Beach Resort is to provide an unparalleled coastal retreat, where guests can escape the demands of daily life and immerse themselves in the beauty and tranquility of our beachfront paradise. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional experiences, fostering environmental stewardship, and contributing positively to our local community.

Olas Tranquilas Vision

At Olas Tranquilas Beach Resort, we envision a sanctuary where the timeless beauty of the natural world harmonizes seamlessly with the pinnacle of human hospitality. Our vision is to be the premier destination for those seeking not just a getaway, but an immersive journey into the tranquility and splendor of coastal living.

High Tides and Good Vibes

Banana Boat

Add excitement to your Olas Tranquilas Beach visit with a thrilling banana boat ride! Perfect for family or friends, it's a memorable group adventure you won't forget.

Parasailing

Enjoy thrilling parasailing along Olas Tranquilas' coast, alone or with a partner. Soar high above the water, towed by a boat, for breathtaking island views.

Flyboarding

The flyboard attaches to your feet and connects to a watercraft via a hose. This allows you to hover above water, much like skating on land.
